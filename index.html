Leonardo da Vinci

Leonardo Da Vinci (1452-1519) was an Italian Renaissance artist and inventor who is widely regarded as one of the greatest mind in history. He was born in the town of Vinci, near Florence, and was apprenticed to the artist Andrea del Verrochio. He later worked in the studios of Lorenzo de' Medici and Francesco del Gioncondo, and was a member of the Guild of St Luke.
